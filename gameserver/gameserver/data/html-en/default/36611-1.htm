Hunting For Sharks
I tried to open access to a new, unexplored continent Gracia , which was hidden from prying eyes behind a veil of thick fog . For this I use the ancient spell binding Ancient Castle on the island with the continent Gracia Parnassus . Lord, I do not imagine that my good intentions disturbed bloodthirsty monsters guarding the limits of ancient territories. These ruthless monster crept into our world and destroyed the portal , and now represent a grave danger to any soldier or traveler. Please help destroy them.
