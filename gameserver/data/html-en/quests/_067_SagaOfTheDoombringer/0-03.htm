!Hierarch Kekropus: Then you must embark upon a journey to unleash your sealed power. Scattered throughout the world there are magical objects that can help unlock your potential called Tablets of Vision. These *6* Tablets of Vision should provide you the insight necessary to break the seal. Visit *Master Duroga in the Town of Aden.* He will give you additional details. On this quest, you will also likely encounter the Guard of Vision, Eranude, who is a kind of watchman for the Tablets of Vision. His task is to test the individuals who approach the Tablets of Vision to ensure they are worthy to communicate with the objects. Approach him with great care!
